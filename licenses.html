Photo Licenses

Understanding photo licenses is crucial when using images. It ensures you have the legal right to use a photo for your intended purpose, whether it's for commercial projects, personal use, or other applications. Different licenses grant varying levels of usage rights, so it's essential to choose the right one to avoid potential legal issues.

Royalty-Free License

Use the photo multiple times for commercial or personal projects without paying royalties. No exclusive rights.

Rights-Managed License

License is granted for a specific use, duration, and region. Pricing varies based on usage.

Creative Commons License

Photos available for free use under specified conditions. Always check attribution requirements.
